Privacy Policy

Missing Vendor Name Checker is an app designed to help merchants identify products that are missing vendor information inside their store.

Data Access

The app accesses the following store data through the Shopify Admin API:

  • Product information (read-only)
  • Store identification information required for authentication

Data Usage

Product data is accessed only to perform on-demand scans initiated by the merchant. Scan results are displayed inside the Shopify Admin interface.

Data Storage

The app stores minimal operational data required for functionality, such as:

  • Store identifier
  • Scan run metadata

Scan metadata is retained only as long as necessary to support app functionality and troubleshooting, and is not used for analytics or profiling.

When the app is uninstalled, any stored operational data associated with the store is deleted.

The app does not store customer personal data and does not modify store data.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell, share, or transfer store data to third parties.

Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, contact the app developer through the app listing.
